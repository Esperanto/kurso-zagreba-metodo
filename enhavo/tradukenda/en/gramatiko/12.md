# *Ju … des*

- *Ju pli longe, des pli bone.* – the longer the better
 

# *Ajn*

- *kiom ajn* – however much
 

# The Prefix *dis-*

denotes seperation, dispersal:

- *__dis__ĵeti* – to scatter
- *__dis__sendi* – to broadcast
 

# The Prefix *-on*

denotes a fraction:

- *du__on__o*   – half
- *tri__on__o*  – third
- *kvar__on__o* – quarter
- *ses__on__o*  – sixth
