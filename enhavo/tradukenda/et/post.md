# After the course

You have learned the basic building blocks of Esperanto. The next step is to use the language with other people.

- [Eventa Servo](https://eventaservo.org/) lists Esperanto meetings and online events around the world.
- [UEA](https://uea.org/) is the Universal Esperanto Association.
- [Amikumu](https://www.amikumu.com/) can help you find Esperanto speakers nearby.

Write a few simple messages, read a little every day, and try speaking with other Esperantists. Regular use will make the language yours.
