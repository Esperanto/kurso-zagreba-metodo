# The Preposition *Da*

is a preposition used in expressions of weight, measure or quantity:

- _kilogramo **da** sukero_ – a kilo of sugar
- _glaso **da** akvo_ – a glass of water
- _multe **da** ideoj_ – a lot of ideas

# The Suffix *-ig*

This "causative" suffix means "to make, cause to be":

- _bel**ig**i_ – to beautify
- _malplen**ig**i_ – to empty
- _san**ig**i_ – to cure, to heal
- _mort**ig**i_ – to kill

# The Suffix *-aĉ*

shows the speaker has a low opinion of the thing in question:

- _hund**aĉ**o_ – cur
- _knab**aĉ**o_ – brat
- _skrib**aĉ**i_ – to scribble
