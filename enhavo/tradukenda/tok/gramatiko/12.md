# *Ju … des*

- *Ju pli longe, des pli bone.* – the longer the better

# *Ajn*

- *kiom ajn* – however much

# The Prefix *dis-*

denotes separation, dispersal:

- _**dis**ĵeti_ – to scatter
- _**dis**sendi_ – to broadcast

# The Suffix *-on*

denotes a fraction:

- _du**on**o_ – half
- _tri**on**o_ – third
- _kvar**on**o_ – quarter
- _ses**on**o_ – sixth
